# Legal notes

TonyST Web is only a shell and integration layer.

Do not distribute commercial Atari ST games unless you have the rights.

Do not distribute copyrighted Atari TOS images unless you have the rights.

EmuTOS is the cleaner open source route for the operating system side, but compatibility may vary by game.

Hatari is open source software. If you distribute a modified or bundled Hatari build, you need to comply with its licence terms.
